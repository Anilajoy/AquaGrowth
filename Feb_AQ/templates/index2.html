Welcome to AquaGrowth Forecast

About the project

AquaGrowth Forecast, a small-scale project contributing to the aquaponics community, offers an online platform for predicting plant growth and monitoring water quality. This initiative aims to serve individuals within the aquaponics community seeking insights into these critical aspects of their systems. Plant growth is determined by parameters such as plant height or diameter, while water quality is assessed through measurements of water TDS or pH levels.
